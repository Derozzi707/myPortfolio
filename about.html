Biography

Founded in 2024 by Derek Iozzio of Phoenix, AZ. Derek got his start in programming through California Institute of Arts and Technology, learning the skills necessary to develop software and technologies in our digital age. He began to work as a freelance web designer, doing front-end work using HTML and CSS, before expanding to full-stack web development, utilizing additional programming languages to begin developing full websites. In the future, he plans to hire on full-time staff for Graphic Design, Photography, and additional team members for web development, to allow for expansion into full-scale software development of web-apps, mobile applications, and beyond. In his free time, he enjoys PC gaming for Rocket League and Hell Let Loose and exploring all genres of music through playing guitar and piano.
